LET'S TALK

About Us
Fuelbee's LET's TALK is an initiative to bring together parents - the nurturers of the future generation, and empower them on various aspects of parenting. We strongly believe that good parenting is not just about catering to the needs of our children, but it's about leaving behind a generation better than ours for social well-being.

PARENTAL GUIDANCE

The rise in need for parental Intervention and Participation

  • Emergence of nuclear family system
  • Excessive exposure to media and technology
  • Fast paced lifestyles
  • Era of cut-throat competitiveness
  • Rise in depression amongst adults & children

This has made parental guidance more significant than ever to ensure a child's physical, psychological & social well-being.
